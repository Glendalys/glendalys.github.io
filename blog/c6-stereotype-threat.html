Stereotype Threat

February 20, 2015

Today I learned what stereotype threat means and realized I can now name something I have felt in my life more than I like to admit. I am a queer woman of color. I grew up in the Bronx and even though I have a graduate degree, speak two languages, and have won national awards I do know and have felt that I have not shown my full abilities under certain situations. I have already felt it at DBC and it does worry me at times. I remember my first GPS. I was anxious the night before and felt very insecure.
